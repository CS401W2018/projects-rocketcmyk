3. What are special entity characters?

4. What are phrase tags?

- Phrase Tags are HTML tags that puts a specific style on words and phrases. An example is making a text bold or italic.

5. What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

- OL is short for Ordered List, with this tag, making a sequential list (either in roman numerals or letters) in HTML can be made possible. On the other hand, UL short for unordered list can be used to make bulleted lists.
