Featured Character of the Day!


Pikachu comes from the game Pokemon. First appearing in Pokemon Red and Blue (1996). Pikachu became the most recognizable Pokemon up to this day. It's popularity due to its charming yellow color and cute design made it famous with kids and preteens.

Pikachu is and electric type Pokemon. In the game, it can be found in the Viridian Forest or in the Powerplant. Some of its popular abilities are Thunderbolt and Volt Tackle Pikachu can evolve into Raichu if given the Thunder stone.
