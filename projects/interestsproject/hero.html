My Hero: Heraclitus

Interest Hero

Heraclitus was an ancient greek from Ephesus. He was also called as "the dark" or "the obscure" because of his teachings that were usually cryptic and paradoxical using wordplay. His teachings were centered on the topic of change and finding unity in the opposites.Moreover, most of his teachings can be interpreted in different ways. The life of Heraclitus is not well documented and only a few of his works have survived. However, his works greatly influenced western philosphy.

Famous Teachings:

Everything Flows
This talks about his core philosphy that everything is constantly changing. Nothing is permanent and the nature of existence is dynamic.
Silence is Healing
Talks about how words can only go far in expressing the deepest truths. Silence can be a way to step back and observe.
We are most nearly ourselves when we achieve the seriousness of the child at play.
Heraclitus points out that the intense focus of a child playing is more authentic than an adult living their day to day lives.
